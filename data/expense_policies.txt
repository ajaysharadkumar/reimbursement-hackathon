# Company Expense Policy

## Travel
- P101: Travel expenses have a maximum allowance of INR 5000 for applicable grades G4 and G5.
- P102: Travel expenses have a maximum allowance of INR 2500 for applicable grades G2 and G3.
- P103: Travel expenses have a maximum allowance of INR 1000 for applicable grade G1.

## Lodging
- P104: Lodging expenses have a maximum allowance of INR 8000 for applicable grades G4 and G5.
- P105: Lodging expenses have a maximum allowance of INR 4000 for applicable grades G2 and G3.
- P106: Lodging expenses have a maximum allowance of INR 2000 for applicable grade G1.

## Meals
- P107: Meals expenses have a maximum allowance of INR 500 for applicable grades G1, G2, G3, G4, and G5.

## Office Supplies
- P108: Office Supplies expenses have a maximum allowance of INR 1000 for applicable grades G3, G4, and G5.
- P109: Office Supplies expenses have a maximum allowance of INR 500 for applicable grades G1 and G2.

## Training
- P112: Training expenses have a maximum allowance of INR 10000 for applicable grades G3, G4, and G5.
- P113: Training expenses have a maximum allowance of INR 5000 for applicable grades G1 and G2.

## Other
- P114: Other expenses have a maximum allowance of INR 750 for applicable grades G1, G2, G3, G4, and G5.
